About Makeroid Builder

No Coding!

Thanks to Makeroid Builder, you can create any app without programming a single line of code

Cloud Based

Makeroid Builder is hosted on Google Cloud Infrastructure. This means that your projects are safely stored on servers, so you won't have to worry about downloading them making backups

Material Design

Our Builder have integrated the official Material Design, providing a very clean and intuitive UI which is very friendly for users.

99.9% of Uptime

We have a powerful infrastructure which will provide 24/7 servers, with an uptime of 99.9%.

Live Testing

Use our Companion to see in real time how your app looks like.

Material Design

All apps made in Makeroid will have Material Design, providing a nice UI for users.

Fully compatible

All Android devices are supported from Android 4.1 JellyBean! There are no restrictions

Incredibly Simple

Use our Block based code system to "program" your app. Just join blocks and your app will be ready!

130+ Components

We have at your disposal more than 130 unique components which will add different features to your app.

Monetization Options

We have several components which will allow you to get benefits from your apps, such us Ads or InApp Purchases.

Much more

Multiple Language

Push Notifications

We provide a Push Notification system powered by OneSignal

In App Purchases

We integrated the native In App Purchases from Google Play Store as a component, allowing you to sell products in your app.

Ad Components

Several types of Ad components are available for you to monetize your app visits! AdMob, Amazon, Facebook, ...; and more coming!

Share Projects

Technical support
